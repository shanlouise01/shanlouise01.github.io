Shannon Lauer is a senior English Literature major with a co-major in Film Studies at Miami University. Her zodiac-inspired poems are a part of her astrology-themed poetry collection that she created for her Capstone class at Miami University. Along with poetry, Shannon enjoys creating art in other forms such as short films, paintings, and choreography.

Shannon is a scorpio with a love for anything related to astrology. She has a Sun in Scorpio, Moon in Libra, and Rising in Cancer.

You can find more of Shannon's work at her website.
