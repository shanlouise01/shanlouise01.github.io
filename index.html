The Zodiacs at a Glance

Get to know the zodiacs!

Aries

March 21st - April 19th

Aries are very passionate and motivated. They are also very confident and determined.

Taurus

April 20th - May 20th

Taurus' are very grounded and practical. They are also very loyal and reliable.

Gemini

May 21st - June 20th

Gemini's are very spontanious and adventurous. They are also playful and curious.

Cancer

June 21st - July 22nd

Cancer's are very emotional and sensitive. They are also very loyal and sympathetic.

Leo

July 23rd - August 22nd

Leo's are very creative and passionate. They are also very confident and outgoing.

Virgo

August 23rd - September 22nd

Virgo's are very loyal and hardworking. They are also very practical and analytical.

Libra

September 23rd - October 22nd.

Libra's are very diplomatic and fair-minded. They are also very social and cooperative.

Scorpio

October 23rd - November 21st

Scorpio's are very passionate and stubborn. They are also very resourceful and brave.

Sagittarius

November 22nd - December 21st

Sagittarius' are very generous and idealistic. They are also very humorous and honest.

Capricorn

December 22nd - January 19th

Capricorn's are very responsible and disciplined. They are also very good managers.

Aquarius

January 20th - February 18th

Aquarius' are very progressive and original. They are also very independent and humanitarian.

Pisces

February 19th - March 20th

Pisces' are very compassionate and artistic. They are also very intuitive and gentle.
